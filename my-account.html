Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use coupons in Store?

If you want to use a coupon code just show the image to the cashier upon check out and the discount will be applied to your purchase.

How do I use a coupon online on Home&KitchenHub?

To apply Home&KitchenHub coupons, first add the items that you want to your cart. When you're done shopping click the "Add to Cart" button in the top right hand corner of the page. On the next page above "Proceed to Checkout” you can enter your promo code or coupon code in the enter promo code box.

How do I keep track of the best deals from Home&KitchenHub?

You can always be kept in the know about upcoming promotions, new product releases and changes to in-store services by signing yourself up for free to our Home&KitchenHub mailing list. You can also check our coupon page for the latest deals!
